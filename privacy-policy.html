FlashForge Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 11, 2026

FlashForge (referred to as "the App") is a professional flashlight, strobe signaling, and Material-designed Morse code utility tool built under the package name com.truelokal.flashforge. We are committed to maintaining the absolute privacy and security of everyone who uses the App.

1. Zero Collection of Personal Information

We believe that your personal data belongs to you. FlashForge does not collect, record, log, store, upload, transmit, or share any personal identity identifiers, location points, phone logs, account information, or usage metadata. The App operates with complete respect for user privacy.

2. Hardware Access & Device Permissions

To provide its core utilities, the App requires access to the following hardware APIs. These are processed entirely locally on-device:

  • Camera Permission (android.permission.CAMERA): Required solely to access the live video viewfinder stream to detect and decode Morse code light signals. No camera frames are stored, captured, video-recorded, or sent over any networks. The analysis occurs purely in volatile RAM and each frame is immediately discarded after mathematical luma calculation.
  • Torch/Flash Control: Required to toggle the camera's LED flash hardware for lighting, SOS signaling, strobe effects, and Morse code transmissions. This permission does not access any user photos, videos, or files.

🔒 Security First: Camera analysis performs purely local mathematical computations on your device. Zero bytes of visual information ever leave your phone.

3. Network Communications

FlashForge operates completely offline. The App does not contain network request code libraries, background analytics tracking, cloud sync pipelines, or advertisement networks. Since it has no internet permissions, it is technically impossible for the App to send any of your information to external servers.

4. Children's Privacy

Since FlashForge collects zero personal data, it is 100% compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) guidelines. It is completely safe for children of all ages.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Since we do not collect any communication details, we cannot notify users individually. We encourage you to check this page periodically for any modifications.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or feedback regarding privacy or the permissions requested by the App, feel free to reach out to us at support@truelokal.com.
